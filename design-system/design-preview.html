Red is reserved for compliance failure on the document and review surfaces — missing mandatory response, word-count breach, unanswered tender question. Never for deletions, never decoratively. Chrome-level destructive actions keep conventional red; the rule protects a context, not a hex value.

Unsourced is marked by form, not by red. A dashed accent outline. Unsourced is unverified, not failed.

Accent never fills a block; alarm always does. The two are adjacent hues, separated by lightness and by form, and never appear in the same component.

Redlines are not red/green. An AI deletion is not an error and an insertion is not a success — they are proposals.
